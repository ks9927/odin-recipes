Mint Chicken

Description

A favorite recipe made with lemon juice and mint leaves. Usually served over white rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup margarine
  • ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
  • ½ cup chopped fresh mint leaves

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mix together the chicken pieces and garlic.
  2. Toss with the flour just to coat, and shake off excess.
  3. Heat the margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the chicken pieces; cook and stir until browned.
  5. Add the mint leaves and lemon juice
  6. Cover and steam for about 5 minutes, just until the chicken is cooked through. Do not over cook the chicken or it will be tough.
