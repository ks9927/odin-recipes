Simple Strawberry Syrup

Description

A simple syrup made of strawberries to use in drinks, desserts, and more

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 cups quartered strawberries

Steps

  1. Combine water and sugar in a saucepan over medium-high heat; stir until sugar is dissolved.
  2. Mix strawberries into saucepan and bring to a boil; boil for 10 minutes.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer until strawberries are mushy and sauce is thick, about 10 minutes.
  4. Strain liquid into a bottle and refrigerate.
